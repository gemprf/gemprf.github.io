2. What Data We Collect

We collect personal data only through direct interactions with our website. Specifically:

  • Support Form Data: When you submit the support form, we collect your name, email address, subject, and message.
  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This includes anonymized information about pages visited, time spent, and general location (country-level).

We do not collect any sensitive data (racial/ethnic origin, religious beliefs, political opinions, biometric data, health data, etc.) unless explicitly and intentionally provided by you in support communications.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your personal data is used only for the following purposes:

  • Responding to Support Requests: To understand your inquiry and provide technical assistance, setup help, and feedback resolution.
  • Communication: To send follow-up messages related to your support request.
  • Website Analytics: To improve our website's functionality and user experience.

We will not use your data for marketing purposes, sell your data, or share it with unrelated third parties without your explicit consent.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases under GDPR:

  • Explicit Consent: For support form submissions, we process your data based on your explicit consent through the consent checkbox in the form.
  • Legitimate Interest: For website analytics, we use legitimate interest to improve our services while respecting your privacy through anonymization and aggregation.

You can withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us, though this will not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined above:

  • Support Form Data: Retained for up to 90 days to allow for follow-up communication and issue resolution. After this period, data is permanently deleted unless you request an extension.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and aggregated analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' default retention policy (14 months).

You may request earlier deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Who Has Access to Your Data

Access to your personal data is strictly limited to:

  • Authorized Team Members: Only members of the GEM-pRF development and support team who need access to respond to your inquiry.
  • Service Providers: Formspree (form submission processor) and Google Analytics, both of which have data processing agreements in place.

Your data is not shared with any other third parties without your explicit consent.

7. Your Data Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten) unless we have a legal obligation to retain it.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we limit how we process your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format for transfer to another service.
  • Right to Object: You can object to specific processing activities.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: If processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at with a clear description of your request. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

  • All form submissions are transmitted via HTTPS encryption
  • Data is stored securely through trusted service providers (Formspree)
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security reviews and updates are conducted

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses the following cookies:

  • Google Analytics Cookies: Used to track website usage and improve user experience. These are analytics cookies that collect anonymized data.

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through browser settings. You can:

  • Disable cookies entirely
  • Allow cookies only from specific sites
  • Clear cookies after each browsing session
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on

Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

11. Children's Privacy

If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will take steps to delete such information promptly. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.
